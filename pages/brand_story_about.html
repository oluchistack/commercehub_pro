Our Journey

Key milestones in building the future of digital commerce

2020

The Vision

Founded with a simple belief: online shopping should feel personal, trustworthy, and delightful. Our founders, Sarah Chen and Marcus Rodriguez, combined their expertise in AI and retail to create something revolutionary.

2021

AI Breakthrough

Launched our proprietary recommendation engine that learns from community behavior, not just individual purchases. This breakthrough made personalization feel genuinely helpful rather than invasive.

2022

Community Growth

Reached 100,000 active community members who contribute reviews, photos, and recommendations. Our user-generated content became the heart of product discovery on the platform.

2023

Sustainability Focus

Achieved carbon-neutral shipping and launched our circular commerce initiative, helping customers buy, sell, and trade pre-loved items. Sustainability became core to our identity.

2024

Global Expansion

Expanded to serve customers in 15 countries while maintaining our commitment to local communities and small businesses. Our marketplace now features over 10,000 unique sellers.
